# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, inclusive, and constructive for everyone, regardless of background, identity, location, language, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and respectful language.
- Assume good intent, while remaining accountable for impact.
- Discuss ideas with evidence and care.
- Make room for cultural nuance and local knowledge.
- Give constructive feedback focused on the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or private datasets
- Misrepresenting research claims or ownership

## Scope

This code applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and public community channels associated with the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior conflicts with this code. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented where appropriate, and focused on maintaining a safe research environment.
