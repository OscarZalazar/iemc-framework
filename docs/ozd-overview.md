# OZD Engine: Public Overview

This document provides a public, high-level overview of OZD Engine as a conceptual research direction. It does not reveal proprietary implementation details, private algorithms, prompts, scoring logic, code, configurations, or internal product architecture.

## What Is OZD Engine?

OZD Engine is a conceptual AI research and product direction focused on multimodal understanding, creative intelligence, and context-aware interpretation. In public terms, it can be described as an effort to make AI systems more aware of how emotion, narrative, culture, and media signals interact.

## What Problem Does It Try to Solve?

Modern AI tools can classify text, images, audio, and video, but they often miss the human reason behind a message:

- Why a video feels funny
- Why a phrase sounds ironic
- Why a visual scene changes the meaning of the text
- Why a message resonates in one culture but not another
- Why emotional meaning changes over time inside a narrative

OZD Engine explores these questions at a conceptual level through multimodal and culturally aware analysis.

IEMC Framework is the public research framework. It defines concepts, terminology, synthetic examples, and future benchmark ideas.
